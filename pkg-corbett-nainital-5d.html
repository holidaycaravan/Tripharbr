Day-by-Day Itinerary
Day
1
Arrive Jim Corbett (Ramnagar)
Transfer from Delhi/Kathgodam · Resort check-in · Bonfire dinner
▼
Your tour manager receives you at Delhi or Kathgodam and transfers you to the jungle resort in Ramnagar — gateway to Jim Corbett National Park. After check-in, settle into the forest atmosphere with an evening jungle walk briefing by a naturalist, followed by a warm bonfire dinner under the stars.
Airport/Station Transfer Resort Check-in Jungle Walk Briefing Bonfire Dinner
Day
2
Corbett Safari Day
Early morning jeep safari · Elephant safari · Wildlife documentary
▼
Rise before dawn for an early morning jeep safari into Dhikala or Bijrani zone — the best time to spot tigers, leopards, elephants, and spotted deer as they emerge in the golden morning light. Return for breakfast, rest in the afternoon, then head out for an elephant safari through the forest buffer zone as the day cools. Evening: wildlife documentary screening at the resort.
Jeep Safari (Dhikala/Bijrani) Tiger & Wildlife Spotting Elephant Safari Breakfast
Day
3
Corbett → Nainital (2.5 hr)
Bird watching · Transfer · Naini Lake boat ride · Mall Road
▼
Start the morning with a guided bird watching walk around the resort — over 600 species have been recorded in Corbett. After breakfast, transfer to Nainital (approximately 2.5 hours) through scenic Kumaon foothills. Check in to your lake-view hotel, then head straight to the iconic Naini Lake for a peaceful rowing boat ride. Spend the evening exploring Mall Road's shops and eateries.
Bird Watching Walk Corbett–Nainital Transfer Lake-View Hotel Check-in Naini Lake Boat Ride Mall Road Breakfast
Day
4
Nainital Sightseeing
Naina Devi Temple · Snow View Point · Tiffin Top · Bhimtal
▼
A full day exploring the best of Nainital: begin at Naina Devi Temple on the lake's north shore, then take the cable car up to Snow View Point (2270 m) for breathtaking Himalayan panoramas. Trek or ride to Tiffin Top for a golden sunset view. Optional afternoon excursion to the serene Bhimtal Lake, a quieter alternative to Naini, set in a forested valley just 22 km away.
Naina Devi Temple Snow View Point (Cable Car) Tiffin Top Sunset Bhimtal Lake (Optional) Breakfast
Day
5
Depart Nainital
Morning lakeside walk · Zoo visit · Transfer to Kathgodam/Delhi
▼
Wake up to a last misty morning lakeside walk around Naini Lake — the mountains reflected perfectly in the still water. Visit Nainital Zoo before check-out to see Himalayan wildlife including snow leopards, wolves, and red pandas. Your tour manager then transfers you to Kathgodam railway station or onward to Delhi, carrying memories of tigers at dawn and lakes at dusk.
Morning Lakeside Walk Nainital Zoo Transfer to Kathgodam/Delhi Breakfast
