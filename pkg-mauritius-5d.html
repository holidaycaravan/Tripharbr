Day-by-Day Itinerary
Day
1
Arrive Mauritius
Airport transfer · Hotel check-in · Beach sunset
▼
Land at Sir Seewoosagur Ramgoolam International Airport and transfer to your hotel on the northwest coast. Check into your beachfront or sea-view room. Spend the evening at Grand Baie — a vibrant resort town with restaurants, bars, and a beach that glows in the setting sun.
Airport TransferHotel Check-inGrand Baie Beach
Day
2
Catamaran Cruise to Île aux Cerfs
Full-day catamaran · Snorkelling · Deer Island beach
▼
Board a luxury catamaran for a full-day island cruise. Snorkel above brilliant coral gardens and tropical fish, enjoy a BBQ lunch served on deck, and spend the afternoon on Île aux Cerfs — a private paradise island with powder-white sand and impossibly clear water. Sunset return to the mainland.
Catamaran CruiseSnorkellingÎle aux Cerfs BeachBreakfast + BBQ Lunch
Day
3
Undersea Walk + Grand Baie
Undersea walk · Glass-bottom boat · Caudan Waterfront
▼
Morning undersea walk at Trou aux Biches — strap on a diving helmet and walk along the seabed at 3–4 m depth, surrounded by fish that eat from your hands (no swimming experience required). Glass-bottom boat ride over the reef. Afternoon at Caudan Waterfront in Port Louis for shopping and a street food lunch.
Undersea WalkGlass-Bottom BoatCaudan WaterfrontBreakfast
Day
4
South Mauritius — Chamarel + Black River Gorges
7-Coloured Earth · Chamarel Waterfall · Rum distillery
▼
Full-day south tour: Chamarel's seven-coloured sand dunes (a geological freak where volcanic sediment created rainbow-hued concentric mounds), the 100 m Chamarel Waterfall, and Black River Gorges National Park — Mauritius's last tract of native forest. End the day at a historic rum distillery for a plantation tour and tasting.
Chamarel 7-Coloured EarthChamarel WaterfallBlack River GorgesRum DistilleryBreakfast
Day
5
Departure
Morning beach · Airport transfer · Fly home
▼
Final morning at your leisure on the beach. Collect sea-glass, pick up vanilla, rum, and wooden handicrafts from the hotel boutique. Transfer to the airport for your flight home, tanned, rested, and already planning your return.
