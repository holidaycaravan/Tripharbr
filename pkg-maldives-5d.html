Day-by-Day Itinerary
Day1
Arrive Maldives — Seaplane / Speedboat Transfer & Villa Check-in
Velana International Airport → Resort
▼
Land at Velana International Airport in Male. Your resort transfer — either a breathtaking seaplane flight (15-30 minutes over atolls and lagoons) or a speedboat — brings you to your island. Check into your overwater villa: glass floor panels in the bedroom, a private deck over turquoise water, and a ladder directly into the lagoon. The rest of the day is yours to swim, snorkel from your villa, or simply sit on your deck and watch the water.
Airport Transfer IncludedVilla Check-inPrivate Deck & Snorkelling
Day2
House Reef Snorkelling & Dolphin Watching Sunset Cruise
Resort Island
▼
Morning snorkelling on the house reef — directly accessible from the beach or your villa ladder. Most Maldivian house reefs have incredible coral gardens with sea turtles, reef sharks, rays, and hundreds of species of colourful fish. Afternoon at leisure by the pool or beach. Evening dolphin watching cruise on a traditional dhoni boat — spinner dolphins are commonly seen, and the sunset from the water is extraordinary.
All Meals IncludedHouse Reef SnorkellingDolphin Watching CruiseSunset on Dhoni
Day3
Excursion Day — Snorkelling Spots & Sandbank Picnic
Island Excursion by Speedboat
▼
A speedboat excursion to the best snorkelling spots in the atoll — coral gardens, manta ray cleaning stations, and reef shark nurseries. Stop at a deserted sandbank for a champagne (or mocktail) picnic — just white sand surrounded by turquoise water. Visit a local inhabited island to see authentic Maldivian life — a contrast to the resort world that most visitors never experience.
All MealsSnorkelling ExcursionSandbank PicnicLocal Island Visit
Day4
Water Sports & Private Beach Dinner
Resort Island
▼
A day of water sports — paddleboarding, kayaking, and windsurfing in the calm lagoon. Most resorts have free non-motorised water sports. For those who want more, jet skiing and parasailing are available. The evening is the most memorable — a private beach dinner arranged by your butler, with a table set on the sand, fairy lights, and the stars and phosphorescent water all around you.
All MealsWater SportsPrivate Beach DinnerStar Gazing
Day5
Final Snorkel & Departure
Resort → Male Airport
▼
A final morning in the lagoon — one last snorkel, one last breakfast on your overwater deck, one last look at the turquoise water. Your resort transfer brings you back to Male airport in time for your departure. The Maldives is the kind of place that changes you — you'll spend the flight home already planning your return.
