Our Story
हमारी कहानी

We Believe Every Trip Should Feel Like It Was Made for You.

हम मानते हैं हर यात्रा ऐसी होनी चाहिए जैसे यह आपके लिए बनी हो।

Tripharbr was born from a simple frustration — most travel companies give you a fixed package, take your money, and leave you to figure out the rest. Delays, wrong hotels, no one to call when things go wrong.

ट्रिप हार्बर एक सरल निराशा से पैदा हुआ — अधिकांश ट्रैवल कंपनियां एक तय पैकेज देती हैं, पैसे लेती हैं और बाकी आप पर छोड़ देती हैं।

We set out to do the opposite. Every trip we plan is built from scratch around the specific family, couple, or team booking it. Not a template. Not a copy-paste itinerary. A genuine, personalised plan — with someone responsible for it from the first call to the last drop-off.

हमने इसका उलटा करने का फैसला किया। हर यात्रा जो हम योजना बनाते हैं वह उस परिवार, जोड़े या टीम के लिए बनाई जाती है।

We're based in Noida, operating pan-India, and growing into international destinations — one perfectly planned trip at a time.

हम नोएडा में स्थित हैं, पूरे भारत में काम करते हैं और अंतरराष्ट्रीय गंतव्यों में बढ़ रहे हैं।

About the Founder

A Decade in the Field Before a Day at the Desk

It started on the ground. In 2015, a young tour manager stepped onto his first bus with Delhi Tourism — responsible for a group of strangers, their luggage, their expectations, and their memories. Over the years that followed, he would do this thousands of times. Families on their first hill station trip. Honeymooners nervous about getting it right. Corporate teams who needed more than just logistics — they needed an experience that would actually bring people together. He saw what worked. He saw what didn't. He learned what travelers actually need versus what most travel companies think they need.

Then came the university years — not just studying, but building. Working across several travel startups in roles that spanned operations, product, and partnerships. Understanding how the industry worked from the inside. Building relationships with hotels, ground operators, and the kind of people who make or break a trip when something goes wrong at 11 PM in a town with no signal. By the time most people are just getting started in travel, the founder of Tripharbr had already handled a thousand trips, managed a hundred crises, and built a network that took a decade to earn.

In 2026, that experience became a company. Not a marketplace. Not an aggregator. Not another platform where your booking is a ticket number and your tour manager is a WhatsApp group. Tripharbr is something different — a personal travel service built on the belief that the person planning your trip should know more about travel than you do, not just more about software. Every itinerary is built by someone who has stood where you're going to stand. Every recommendation comes from someone who has eaten at that restaurant, stayed in that hotel, and navigated that mountain road. That's not a marketing line. That's ten years of work.

"I didn't start a travel company because I saw a market opportunity. I started it because I knew exactly what was missing — and I'd spent a decade figuring out how to provide it."

— Founder, Tripharbr
