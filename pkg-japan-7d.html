Day-by-Day Itinerary
Day
1
Arrive Tokyo
Airport pickup · Shinjuku check-in · Shibuya Crossing
▼
Land at Narita or Haneda and be met by your tour manager. Transfer to your hotel in Shinjuku — Tokyo's buzzing heart. After checking in, head to the iconic Shibuya Crossing — the world's busiest pedestrian scramble. Explore the neon-lit streets and end the night with ramen at a local favourite.
Airport Transfer Hotel Check-in Shibuya Crossing
Day
2
Tokyo Sightseeing
Senso-ji · Harajuku · Shinjuku Gyoen · Tokyo Skytree
▼
Full Tokyo day: morning at Senso-ji temple in Asakusa with its giant lantern and incense smoke, afternoon through Harajuku's wild Takeshita Street, then the Shinjuku Gyoen park. Evening ascent of Tokyo Skytree (634m) for a 360° panorama of the city below.
Senso-ji Harajuku Tokyo Skytree Breakfast
Day
3
Mt Fuji Day Trip
Fuji Five Lakes · Chureito Pagoda · Lake Kawaguchi
▼
Early morning departure for the iconic Mt Fuji area. The Chureito Pagoda viewpoint — Japan's most photographed scene — frames Fuji-san perfectly against the sky. Lake Kawaguchi offers boat rides with reflections of the peak. If visibility allows, ascend to the 5th Station (seasonal). Return to Tokyo in the evening.
Mt Fuji Chureito Pagoda Lake Kawaguchi Day Excursion
Day
4
Tokyo → Kyoto (Shinkansen)
Bullet train experience · Nishiki Market · Gion evening walk
▼
Board the legendary Shinkansen bullet train from Tokyo to Kyoto — 2.5 hours of countryside flying past the window at 320 km/h. Arrive in ancient Kyoto and check in. Afternoon at Nishiki Market (Kyoto's Kitchen) for street tastings. Evening stroll through Gion, the geisha district, as lanterns light up the flagstone paths.
Shinkansen Nishiki Market Gion Breakfast
Day
5
Kyoto Temples & Arashiyama
Fushimi Inari · Kinkaku-ji · Arashiyama Bamboo Grove · Monkey Park
▼
Kyoto's full-day highlight run: the mesmerising tunnel of 10,000 torii gates at Fushimi Inari Taisha (start early to beat crowds), the gold-leaf Kinkaku-ji temple reflected in its mirror pond, and the ethereal Arashiyama Bamboo Grove. Optional: Arashiyama Monkey Park for a wild detour.
Fushimi Inari Kinkaku-ji Bamboo Grove Breakfast
Day
6
Kyoto → Nara → Osaka
Nara deer park · Todai-ji · Dotonbori food street · Osaka Castle
▼
A triangular day trip: first to Nara, where over 1,000 sacred deer roam freely and bow for shika senbei crackers. Visit the colossal Todai-ji Buddha Hall (the world's largest wooden building). Then drive on to Osaka — check in and walk Dotonbori for takoyaki, okonomiyaki, and the iconic Glico Running Man sign.
Transfer Nara Deer Park Todai-ji Dotonbori Breakfast + Street Food
Day
7
Osaka & Departure
Osaka Castle · Kuromon Market · Kansai Airport
▼
Morning at Osaka Castle — Japan's most recognisable landmark, surrounded by a moat and cherry trees. Browse Kuromon Ichiba Market (Osaka's Kitchen) for one last round of street food. Your tour manager handles a smooth transfer to Kansai International Airport for your flight home.
Osaka Castle Kuromon Market Kansai Airport
