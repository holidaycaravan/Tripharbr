How Much Does a Goa Trip Actually Cost in 2026?

Real numbers for every budget — from ₹8,000 per person weekend trips to ₹50,000+ luxury escapes.

Quick AnswerA 5-day Goa trip costs ₹8,000–₹25,000 per person (hotel, food, activities) excluding flights. Flights from Delhi add ₹4,000–₹10,000 per person return. Total per person for a mid-range 5-day Goa trip including flights from Delhi: ₹18,000–₹35,000. Goa is one of India’s most affordable beach destinations when planned well.

How much do flights to Goa cost?

Delhi/Mumbai to Goa (Dabolim or Mopa airport) return per person:

  • Budget (IndiGo, SpiceJet): ₹4,000–₹8,000 from Delhi
  • From Mumbai: ₹2,500–₹5,000
  • From Bengaluru: ₹2,000–₹4,500
  • Peak season (December–January): Double these prices — book 3–4 months ahead
  • Train from Delhi to Goa: ₹1,500–₹3,000 per person (sleeper/3AC) — takes 24–28 hours but very scenic

How much does accommodation in Goa cost?

Per room per night:

  • Budget guesthouses (North Goa, near beach): ₹800–₹2,000
  • 3-star hotels: ₹2,500–₹5,000
  • 4-star beach resorts: ₹5,000–₹12,000
  • 5-star luxury resorts: ₹12,000–₹40,000+
  • Private villas (groups): ₹8,000–₹25,000 per night (entire villa)

Best value: 3-star hotel in Candolim or South Goa — good quality, close to beach, ₹3,000–₹5,000 per night.

How much does food cost in Goa?

  • Beach shack fish thali: ₹200–₹350 per person (best meal in Goa)
  • Mid-range restaurant: ₹500–₹1,000 per person per meal
  • Resort restaurant: ₹800–₹1,500 per person
  • Fine dining (Thalassa, Gunpowder): ₹2,000–₹4,000 per person
  • Daily food budget per person: ₹600–₹2,000

How much do Goa activities cost?

  • Parasailing: ₹600–₹1,000 per person
  • Jet skiing (15 mins): ₹500–₹800
  • Banana boat: ₹300–₹500 per person
  • Scuba diving (beginner, 1 dive): ₹3,500–₹5,000
  • Old Goa church visit: Free
  • Spice plantation tour with lunch: ₹600–₹900 per person
  • Dudhsagar Falls jeep safari: ₹2,500–₹3,500 per jeep
  • Sunset cruise: ₹500–₹1,500 per person

Is Goa Good Value?

Goa is exceptional value for a beach holiday — one of the most affordable coastal destinations in Asia. The fish thalis, the sunsets, the heritage of Old Goa, and the range of resorts at every price point make it accessible to everyone. The only time Goa becomes expensive is December–January peak season without advance booking.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Goa expensive in December?
Yes — December and January are peak season. Hotel prices are 2–3x the off-season rates. Book 3–4 months ahead for December. October and February are our top recommendations for value.
How much does a Goa trip cost for a group of 10?
A private villa for a group of 10 (4–5 rooms or an entire villa) costs ₹8,000–₹20,000 per night for the villa. Per person, a 4-day group Goa trip costs ₹10,000–₹18,000 inclusive — significantly cheaper per person than traveling as a couple.
Is Goa cheaper than Thailand?
Yes — significantly. A 5-day Goa trip costs ₹15,000–₹30,000 per person. A 5-day Thailand trip costs ₹25,000–₹50,000 per person. Goa wins on cost.
What is the cheapest month to visit Goa?
September and early October — just after monsoon, beaches freshly cleaned, fewer tourists, lowest prices. Weather is transitional but perfectly acceptable.
How much should I budget for nightlife in Goa?
Entry to most Goa beach clubs: ₹500–₹2,000 (often includes drinks). Drinks at beach clubs: ₹300–₹700 each. Budget ₹2,000–₹5,000 per person per evening if you plan to go out.
